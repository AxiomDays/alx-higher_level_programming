Add two integers.

Parameters:
- a (int): The first integer.
- b (int): The second integer.

Returns:
int: The sum of a and b.

Examples:
>>> add_integer(2, 3)
5

>>> add_integer(-1, 5)
4

>>> add_integer(0, 0)
0

>>> add_integer(2.1, 4)
6
>>> add_integer(5, 7.8)
12

>>> add_integer(2.45, 3.1)
5
>>> [add_integer(i, i ** 2) for i in range(6)]
[0, 2, 6, 12, 20, 30]
